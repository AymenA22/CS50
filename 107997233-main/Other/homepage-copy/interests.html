Nissan Skyline - RB26DETT

Description:

The RB26DETT engine was one of the most advanced engines of its time. Used in three generations of the Nissan Skyline GTR ranging from the GTR32 August 1989 – November 1994, to the GTR33 January 1995 – November 1998 and finally ending in the GTR34 January 1999 – August 2002 with an additional Z-Tune model from 2003–2007.
