CS50x Journey

After 10 years in the field of mechanical engineering, specifically as a design engineer, the pull of computer science became something that I strongly believe will support my engineering career and (hopefully) expand my opportunities.
I think it goes without saying that a Harvard's CS50x computer science course is a challenge for most people, and I am no exception. The 11 week course has taken quite significantly longer, however, gaining a full understanding of each chapter has been my goal.
In the table below, I have listed each chapter that I completed within 2023/2024 sylabus and have given a brief summary of each chapter.
